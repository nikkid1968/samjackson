About Samuel L Jackson

Born on December 21, 1948, in Washington, D.C.,
Samuel L. Jackson graduated from Morehouse College in 1972 and began his
acting career on stage. A move to New York City led to a friendship
with Spike Lee and his first movie gigs, before he enjoyed a
star-making turn with a commanding performance in Pulp Fiction. Jackson became a ubiquitous presence on the big screen,
appearing in big-budget films like Star Wars: The Phantom Menace
and its sequels, and the numerous installments of the Marvel
Cinematic Universe. In 2011 he was named the highest grossing actor of all time with more than $7.2 billion in wealth.
